Mission Statement

We believe founders and developers should focus on the core product without distractions. However, a great product without proper marketing is a great product you’ve never heard of. Marketing done right is both an art and a science, and is often hard to set up and scale at the early stages of the project’s lifecycle. We are here to fix this and make sure that your idea finds its adopters.

Our approach

We strongly believe that our readers deserve only the very best. That is why all sponsored content has to comply with the guidelines established by Cointelegraph Media Group. The three main pillars on which we put our standards for sponsored content are transparency, independence, and editorial discretion.

The sponsored content is produced only by a separate advertorial team in an unbiased and objective manner. Editorial discretion means that Cointelegraph has a right to reject any advertiser or sponsored content piece which could compromise the Cointelegraph brand
